Can we divide Open Access policies into types?

PASTEUR4OA have been working on the creation of a typology for the Open Access policies within ROARMAP. The types are defined by the answers to the questions asked when adding a policy to ROARMAP.

The current typology has the following types: 1A, 1B, 1C, 2A, 2B, 2C, 3 and 4.
